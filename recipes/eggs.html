How to make Eggs

Making Eggs now a days is a very simple task. With just a few ingredients you should be enjoying your Eggs in no time.

Ingredients

  • Two Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. heat up pan, and add oil
  2. When hot add two eggs into pan and wait until finished
  3. Once done and you have removed from pan onto plate, add salt and pepper
